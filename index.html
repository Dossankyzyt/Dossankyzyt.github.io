Project Abstract

This project identifies and fills knowledge gaps in Wikidata related to the Russian city of Tver (Q2288), focusing on its geographic and political relationships, including missing river connections and local government data.

Rivers of Tver

Tver is located at the confluence of the Volga, Tvertsa, and Tmaka rivers. Our research showed that Wikidata lacked links to Tvertsa (Q671890) and Tmaka (Q4467262) via property P206.

Methodology

Using SPARQL queries and LLMs, we identified missing data and generated RDF triples to enrich Wikidata. Both Gemini and ChatGPT were used for validation.

Outcomes

  • Improved geographic accuracy of Tver's Wikidata entry
  • Reusable method for identifying missing links (P206)
  • Supports downstream applications and open knowledge quality
