Project Abstract

This project identifies and fills knowledge gaps in Wikidata related to the Russian city of Tver (Q2288), focusing on its geographic and political relationships. We enriched the graph by adding missing connections to rivers such as the Tvertsa and Tmaka, as well as political leadership using the property P6 (head of government).
