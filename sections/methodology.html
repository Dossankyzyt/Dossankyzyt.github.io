üß© Gap Identification

Our project began with the observation that Wikidata, while structured and community-driven, often lacks complete geographic and civic connections. In the case of Tver (Q2288), we identified two key gaps:

  • Missing river connections: Tver lies on three rivers (Volga, Tvertsa, Tmaka), but only the Volga is linked via P206.
  • Incomplete mayoral data: Mayors were either missing or inconsistently represented via P6 ("head of government") or P39 ("position held").

We began by identifying gaps in Tver‚Äôs Wikidata entry, focusing on:

SPARQL queries were used to examine relationships involving properties:

  • P206: located in or next to body of water
  • P6 / P39: head of government / position held

üß† 2. Validation with Large Language Models (LLMs)

We used ChatGPT and Gemini to:

  • Cross-check which rivers flow through Tver
  • Identify potential mayors of Tver
  • Generate RDF triples to enrich Wikidata

LLM outputs were validated against Wikidata and corrected (e.g., QID fixes).
